Dycom Memory Game

Instructions

Goal:

Your goal is to match all the cards on the board in the fewest number of tries.

How to Play:

Click on a card to reveal its image. Click on another card to reveal its image. If the images match, the cards will remain face up. If the images don't match, the cards will flip back over. Remember their positions for future turns. Keep matching pairs until all cards are face up.

Difficulty Levels:

Choose your preferred difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard. Each difficulty level determines the number of cards on the board. Harder levels have more cards, making the game more challenging.

Leaderboard:

Optionally, you can enter your name before starting the game. Your name will be displayed on the leaderboard if you achieve a high score. Compete with friends and colleagues to see who can complete the game with the fewest tries!

Start Game:

Click the "Play" button to begin playing after selecting your difficulty level. Have fun and enjoy testing your memory skills with Dycom's Memory Game!
